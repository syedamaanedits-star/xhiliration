ABOUT X-HILIRATION

X-Hiliration is Xavier University Patna's flagship inter-college sports festival, bringing together 20+ colleges from five states. The fest promotes physical fitness, teamwork, leadership, and sportsmanship, culminating in a grand Valedictory Ceremony, EDM Night, and Live Band Performance — aligning with the Government of Bihar's vision for youth engagement and sporting culture.
